    List of other places you can visit in Singapore
  • Tiger Sky Tower
    • The highest observation tower in Singapore
  • Henderson Waves
    • It crosses over Henderson Road and connects Mount Faber Park to Telok Blangah Hill Park.
  • Sentosa
    • The island is separated from the main island of Singapore by a channel of water, the Keppel Harbour, and is adjacent to Pulau Brani, a smaller island wedged between Sentosa and the main island.
  • Singapore Art Museum
    • The Singapore Art Museum is an art museum located in the Downtown Core district of Singapore
  • Gardens by the Bay
    • The Gardens by the Bay is a nature park spanning 101 hectares in the Central Region of Singapore, adjacent to the Marina Reservoir.
